About CyberCloud9

CyberCloud9 may be a new company, but it’s built on over 25 years of experience in education.

  With a strong background in teaching and national curriculum design including direct work with the Scottish Qualifications Authority (SQA) we understand how to create learning that is both engaging and impactful. 

The growing demand for cybersecurity skills inspired the launch of CyberCloud9. 

We are a consultancy dedicated to helping schools, colleges and organisations bring digital and cyber education to life.

Our goal is to make sure every learner has the skills and awareness to thrive in a digital world.

At Your Service

Training

We provide bespoke cybersecurity training and workshops tailored to your needs. Sessions can be delivered at your site, remotely, or at our offices in Glasgow. Our hands-on approach ensures learners gain practical skills they can immediately apply.

Curriculum Development

We help design and deliver future-ready learning programmes. From creating innovative content to aligning with industry standards, our consultancy ensures your curriculum is relevant, compliant, and impactful, giving learners the skills that matter most in today’s digital world.

Cyber Awareness Sessions

Our engaging, hands-on sessions teach good cyber hygiene and security practices. Perfect for schools and organisations, these sessions make learning practical, relevant, and fun, helping participants understand how to protect themselves and their systems.

Pentesting Services

We carry out professional penetration testing to identify vulnerabilities and strengthen your security. Protect your organisation, your data and what matters most with our thorough, expert-led testing services. Protecting what you love most, your business.
